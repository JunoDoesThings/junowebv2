Venom Energy: Fruit Punch 8/10 (Juno)

Posted on May 16, 2026

Wow!, something other than monster. This drink kinda suprised me! it reminds me of a less sweet pipeline punch with a more natural fruit taste. i REALLY enjoy this flavor! The sweetness and the pure fruit flavor is not too "pushy" and tastes amazing. it leaves a slight fruity after taste that goes away in a minute or so, so its not a issue. im looking forward to trying more venom energy drinks in the future!

8/10, Pretty Fucking Good!
